Our Story

We started Watchtower AB because we saw people getting lost trying to find events in their communities, and organizers struggling to reach the right audiences.

The Problem We're Solving

People want to find events in their area, but they get lost in social media feeds and scattered information. Meanwhile, event organizers can't reach the people who would actually be interested in attending.

Our Approach

We built TodoEvents to solve the "lost and looking" problem. By creating a dedicated space for local event discovery with clear location and category filters, we help people find what they're looking for.

What We're Building

Beyond TodoEvents, we're working on MissionOps - an intelligent planning tool for teams managing complex projects. We believe software should be practical and solve real problems.

Our Philosophy

We're a lean team focused on building tools that work. We believe in honest communication about what we're developing, clear pricing, and helping our users succeed.
